!
Activation friction: RevPoints requires manual opt-in. No evidence of a sustained re-engagement campaign targeting the 53M dormant base
!

!
Earn rate changes are silent: partner shop rates change without proactive comms, eroding trust among power users per community feedback
!
Travel framing dominates messaging: airline miles speak to a minority of users; everyday Revolut Pay redemptions are under-communicated

Messaging gap — current vs recommended
Shifting the narrative
RevPoints' visible marketing currently leans toward travel rewards for high-tier users. The larger untapped audience needs a different angle — one that makes activation feel like a no-brainer for everyday spenders, not just frequent flyers.
Current framing (observed)
"Earn up to 1pt per £1 and redeem for flights" — aspirational, skewed toward Ultra and travel-heavy users
→
Recommended shift
"You are already spending — you should already be earning." Lead with the activation gap as the story; frictionless urgency for the dormant base
Current redemption focus
Airline miles and hotel stays — high aspiration, low frequency for most users
→
Recommended shift
Revolut Pay discounts and partner shops — immediate weekly gratification that drives habitual engagement, not just holiday spending

Proposed 30-60-90 day plan — click each phase
What I would do in the first 90 days
Click each phase to explore the plan. Each phase builds on the previous one — listen first, then test, then scale.
Listen and map
Funnel audit
Map the full activation journey
Identify exactly where in the opt-in flow users drop off. Every touchpoint from app home to RevPoints active state gets documented and prioritised by drop-off rate.
Segmentation
Profile the 53M dormant users
Work with data team to segment dormant users by plan type, spend level, and tenure. Identify highest-propensity first wave — likely active spenders on Plus or Metal plans.
Competitive intel
Audit how competitors talk about loyalty
Document Amex, Chase, and Monzo paid and organic messaging across channels. Identify vocabulary, emotional hooks, and audience framing that RevPoints is not yet using.
Internal alignment
Stakeholder interviews
Meet with product, data, comms, and design teams. Understand what has been tried, what is on the roadmap, and where marketing is blocked vs. where it can move fast.
Test and build
Campaign
Launch activation campaign v1
In-app push and email to the highest-propensity dormant segment. A/B test two frames: "you are missing out" (loss aversion) vs. "here is what you could already have earned" (aspiration). Measure activation rate per variant.
Content
Partner-led social content series
Leverage the Audi F1 and Manchester City partnerships to build organic short-form content that frames RevPoints as a lifestyle product, not a banking feature. Target the 18-34 segment where Revolut already has strong penetration.
Trust
Earn rate transparency protocol
Propose a comms standard for partner rate changes — proactive in-app notification before changes go live. Addresses a known pain point in the loyalty community and protects brand trust long-term.
Positioning
Reframe messaging for Standard users
Develop a messaging track specifically for the Standard plan base — focusing on Revolut Pay discounts and partner shops as low-bar, immediate redemptions. Test in CRM first, then in-product.
Scale and measure
Scale
Roll out winning activation variant
Expand the best-performing message from days 31-60 to the full dormant segment across three or more markets. Localise copy for cultural relevance while maintaining core message consistency.
Education
Launch RevPoints 101 content series
Short-form video series (60-90 seconds) explaining earn and redeem mechanics in plain language. Target Standard users specifically. Distribute across in-app, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts — formats with proven ROI for this age group.
Measurement
Establish PMM-owned KPI framework
Define activation rate, first redemption rate, and upgrade conversion as the three core metrics owned by the PMM function. Build a reporting cadence that connects marketing activity directly to these outcomes.
Strategy
Present H2 recommendations to leadership
Synthesise 90 days of data and testing into a data-backed proposal for H2 2026 — including market prioritisation, channel mix, and any product marketing blockers that need resolution upstream.
